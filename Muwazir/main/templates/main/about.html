About us

Welcome to Muwazir

platform that connects Hajj and Umrah performers with trusted volunteers in the holy sites, to provide assistance and support during the Hajj and Umrah journey.

Our Vision:

We believe that Hajj and Umrah are great spiritual journeys, but they can be physically exhausting, especially for the elderly or people who need special help.

Our goal:

We have developed Muwazir to be a safe and easy-to-use platform, allowing pilgrims to find reliable volunteers to help them perform rituals in comfort and safety.

Values we believe in:

✅Security and trust

All volunteers go through a verification process to ensure their reliability.

✅Cooperation and assistance

We seek to make the Hajj and Umrah journey easier through mutual support.

✅Ease of use

A simple and effective platform to connect pilgrims with volunteers quickly and easily.

Who can benefit from our website?

Hajj and Umrah performers who need help during rituals. Volunteers who want to help and serve the guests of God's house.

How does the website works?

  • Volunteers register on the site and provide their information and expertise.
  • user search for volunteers according to their skills and availability
  • The user can book the appropriate volunteer and communicate with him directly.
  • After the end of service, the user can evaluate the volunteer to help others choose the best volunteer.

"If you are a Hajj or Umrah performer looking for help, or a volunteer who wants to lend a helping hand and serve the guests of the House of God, you can register now and start your journey with us!"
